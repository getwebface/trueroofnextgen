Templestowe is renowned for its expansive properties, rolling hills, and proximity to the Yarra River parklands. This unique mix of topography and heavy tree coverage creates a dual challenge for the area's **terracotta and concrete tiled roofs**, many of which date back to the 1980s.

In the leafy pockets bordering the parklands, constant shade encourages heavy moss and lichen growth. These root systems bite into tile surfaces and mortar, leading to rapid degradation. On the elevated hills, homes are exposed to wind-driven rain that aggressively tests aging ridge cap mortar. We specialize in addressing these specific environmental pressures faced by Templestowe properties.

Our tailored restoration for Templestowe homes includes:
