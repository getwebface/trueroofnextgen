Eltham is known for its stunning natural environment and heavily treed, winding streets. While the leafy canopy is beautiful, it creates a constant battle for the **terracotta and concrete tiled roofs** that sit beneath it. Constant shade and falling debris create the perfect microclimate for heavy moss and lichen growth.

If left unchecked, these root systems bite into the surface of your tiles and mortar, leading to rapid degradation and leaks. We don't just wash the dirt away; our specialized tiled roof restoration process is designed to completely eradicate organic growth and seal your roof against the Eastern suburbs' unique environment.
