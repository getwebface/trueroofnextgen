Kilmore is Victoria's oldest inland town, boasting a unique blend of heritage housing and new developments. Because of this rich history, many homes feature older terracotta and concrete tiled roofs that have endured decades of local weather conditions. As these tiles age, they require specialized care to maintain their structural integrity and appearance.

Our focus in Kilmore is on comprehensive roof restoration. We provide deep cleaning to remove moss and lichen built up over the years, precise re-pointing and re-bedding of ridge caps to ensure your home remains watertight, and application of high-quality protective coatings. Whether you are preserving the classic look of a heritage property or refreshing a more recent build, our restorative process ensures your tiled roof is ready to face the future without compromising the town's historic charm.
