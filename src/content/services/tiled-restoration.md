A full restoration is the most effective way to extend the life of your tiled roof without the massive expense of a replacement. Our proven 4-stage process includes:

1. **High-Pressure Clean:** We remove decades of moss, lichen, and dirt using specialized high-pressure equipment.
2. **Structural Repairs:** We replace all cracked or broken tiles and check all valley irons and flashings.
3. **Re-bedding & Flexi-Pointing:** We strip out old, crumbling mortar and replace it with a mechanical bedding and modern flexible pointing compound that won't crack with house movement.
4. **Protective Coating (Concrete Only):** For concrete tiles, we apply a multi-coat membrane system that restores color and provides a long-lasting weather seal.
