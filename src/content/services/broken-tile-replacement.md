A single cracked tile might seem insignificant, but it acts as a funnel during Melbourne downpours, channeling water directly into your ceiling cavity, rotting structural timber, and ruining plasterboard.

Finding an exact match for a weathered, 40-year-old terracotta or concrete tile is an art form. General handymen often use mismatched profiles or rely on silicone patches that degrade in the sun. We specialize in precision structural repairs, a critical component of our 4-Stage Restoration Process.

**The True Roof Tile Replacement Protocol**

<div class="grid grid-cols-1 md:grid-cols-2 gap-4 mt-8 mb-8">

<div class="glass-card" style="padding: 1.5rem;">

**Expert Sourcing & Matching:** We maintain an extensive network of salvage yards across Victoria. We don't just find a tile that "fits"; we source tiles that match the specific age, profile, and weathering of your existing roof to maintain aesthetic continuity.

</div>

<div class="glass-card" style="padding: 1.5rem;">

**Safe Extraction & Installation:** Old tiles are brittle. Inexperienced foot traffic often breaks three tiles for every one they fix. Our technicians use specialized load-displacement methods to safely extract the broken tile and interlock the replacement without damaging surrounding areas.

</div>

<div class="glass-card" style="padding: 1.5rem;">

**Sarking & Batten Inspection:** When a tile is removed, it opens a window into your roof cavity. We take this opportunity to inspect the underlying timber battens and waterproof sarking for hidden water damage or rot.

</div>

<div class="glass-card" style="padding: 1.5rem;">

**Holistic Restoration Focus:** We view broken tiles as a symptom, not just a problem. While replacing the tiles, we assess the overall condition of your roof, identifying failing mortar or rusted valleys that may require attention to guarantee a watertight home.

</div>

### Stop the Drip Before It Destroys Your Ceiling

Don't let a $50 broken tile cause $5,000 in internal water damage. If you've found pieces of tile in your gutters, or you can see visible cracks from the ground, immediate action is required.
