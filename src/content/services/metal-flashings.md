The most common source of leaks in a tiled roof isn't the tiles themselves, but the metal components that bridge the gaps between tiles and other structures. We specialize in:

<div class="grid grid-cols-1 md:grid-cols-2 gap-4 mt-8">

<div class="glass-card" style="padding: 1.5rem;">

- **Chimney Flashings:** Replacing old, cracked lead or rusted metal flashings around chimneys to stop persistent leaks.

</div>

<div class="glass-card" style="padding: 1.5rem;">

- **Valley Iron Replacement:** Swapping out rusted or blocked valley irons with new, high-sided Colorbond or galvanized valleys.

</div>

<div class="glass-card" style="padding: 1.5rem;">

- **Wall & Apron Flashings:** Ensuring a watertight seal where your roof meets a vertical wall or second story.

</div>

<div class="glass-card" style="padding: 1.5rem;">

- **Box Gutter Maintenance:** Cleaning and repairing internal box gutters to prevent overflow into your ceiling.
