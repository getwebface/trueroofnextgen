Sarking—the reflective foil insulation installed under your roof tiles—is your home's critical secondary line of defense against severe Melbourne weather. Over decades, old sarking can become brittle, tear, or sag, allowing wind-driven rain and dust to breach your ceiling cavity.

Rather than ignoring failing sarking until it causes significant water damage, True Roof integrates targeted sarking repair and replacement into our comprehensive 4-Stage Restoration Process.

**Our Approach to Sarking Maintenance**

We specialize in localized, accessible sarking repairs for tiled roofs. When conducting tile repairs or high-pressure cleaning, our experienced technicians identify areas where your sarking has failed and implement effective, long-lasting solutions.

**Targeted Replacement:** We safely remove the tiles above the damaged section, replace the compromised foil with modern, high-grade heavy-duty sarking, and meticulously relay the tiles.

<div class="glass-card" style="padding: 1.5rem;">

**Condensation Management:** Proper sarking isn't just about stopping leaks—it's vital for managing condensation. We ensure new sarking is installed with correct overlap and tension to direct moisture straight into your gutters.

</div>

<div class="glass-card" style="padding: 1.5rem;">

**Storm Defence:** Intact sarking acts as a powerful barrier against wind-driven rain during Melbourne's notorious sudden squalls, protecting your electrical wiring and plasterboard.

</div>

<div class="glass-card" style="padding: 1.5rem;">

**Thermal Efficiency:** Modern reflective foil significantly improves your home's thermal efficiency, bouncing radiant heat away in summer and retaining warmth during winter.

</div>
