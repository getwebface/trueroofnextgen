Moss, lichen, and embedded grime don't just make your home look neglected—they are actively destroying it. Over time, root systems from organic growth burrow into the porous surface of terracotta and concrete tiles. As they expand with moisture, they create micro-cracks, turning a watertight surface into a sponge that slowly compromises your ceiling.

A standard dirt wash from a general handyman with a pressure washer is a superficial solution. It strips away the surface layer but leaves the root systems intact, while often blasting water directly under your flashings and into your roof cavity.

**The True Roof Biological Decontamination Process**
