A standard tiled roof leak repair in Melbourne typically costs between $350 and $850. This usually covers replacing up to 10 broken tiles, minor mortar patching, or clearing blocked valley irons. Complex leaks involving rusted valley iron replacement or extensive structural damage can exceed $1,500.

Finding the true source of a roof leak is often more difficult than the repair itself, as water can travel meters along rafters before dripping through your ceiling. When we inspect a leak in [Melbourne](/locations/melbourne), we don't just patch the symptom; we diagnose the root cause—whether that's degraded mortar requiring [ridge cap re-bedding and pointing](/services/ridge-cap-rebedding-pointing), or [rusted valley irons](/services/metal-flashings) that need complete replacement.

If your roof is experiencing multiple, recurring leaks, isolated repairs become uneconomical. In these cases, a full [4-stage tiled restoration](/services/tiled-restoration) is often the most cost-effective long-term solution to reset your roof's lifespan and ensure your home remains completely watertight.
