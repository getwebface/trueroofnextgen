Our ridge cap re-bedding and flexi-pointing process is designed to permanently secure the most vulnerable parts of your tiled roof against severe weather conditions.

If you just re-bed the tiles, the rigid sand and cement mortar will inevitably crack over time as the home structure moves, eventually leading to more leaks. If you just patch over failing mortar with pointing, the underlying foundation is still weak.

This is why True Roof always pairs proper mortar re-bedding with a premium flexible pointing finish. It ensures the ridge caps remain completely watertight while having the elasticity to handle natural expansion and contraction, creating a permanent, leak-free solution.
