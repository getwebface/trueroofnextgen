Professional high-pressure tiled roof cleaning in Melbourne costs between $600 and $1,200. This price depends on the steepness, square meterage, and the volume of heavy moss or lichen. Attempting this without commercial equipment often leaves roots behind, causing immediate regrowth.

A proper [high-pressure roof cleaning](/services/cleaning) is not just about aesthetics; it is the critical first stage of any successful [4-stage tiled restoration process](/services/tiled-restoration). If the porous surface of concrete or terracotta tiles isn't thoroughly stripped of organic matter and old, flaking paint, the protective membrane coatings applied later will simply peel off.

Whether you're in [Melbourne](/locations/melbourne) or [Craigieburn](/locations/craigieburn), investing in a professional clean ensures your roof looks fantastic and remains structurally sound for years to come.
