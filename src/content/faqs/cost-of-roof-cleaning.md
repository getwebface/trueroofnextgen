A comprehensive biological decontamination for a Melbourne tiled roof typically requires an investment of $600 to $1,200, depending on the pitch, square meterage, and severity of the biological growth. A superficial spray from a general handyman might seem cheaper, but it leaves the embedded root systems intact inside your porous tiles. Our calibrated commercial extraction process permanently eliminates these roots, preventing immediate regrowth and protecting your home's structural integrity.

A proper [high-pressure roof decontamination](/services/cleaning) is not just about aesthetics; it is the critical first stage of any successful [4-stage tiled restoration process](/services/tiled-restoration). If the porous surface of concrete or terracotta tiles isn't thoroughly stripped of organic matter and failing surface coatings, the protective membrane applied later will simply peel off.

Whether you're in [Melbourne](/locations/melbourne) or [Craigieburn](/locations/craigieburn), investing in a professional clean ensures your roof looks fantastic and remains structurally sound for years to come.
