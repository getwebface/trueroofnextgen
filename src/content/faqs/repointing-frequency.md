In Melbourne, a tiled roof should be repointed every 10 to 15 years. Harsh weather, including intense sun and heavy rain, causes the mortar to crack and degrade. Regular repointing using modern flexible pointing prevents water leaks and secures loose ridge capping.

When the original cement mortar breaks down, it exposes your home to water damage, structural issues, and pest entry. As part of a thorough [roof restoration](/services/tiled-restoration), our team professionally grinds away the old mortar before performing comprehensive [ridge cap re-bedding and pointing](/services/ridge-cap-rebedding-pointing) to ensure long-lasting durability.

Whether you're located in [Melbourne](/locations/melbourne) or [Broadford](/locations/broadford), staying on top of your roof's maintenance timeline saves you from costly emergency repairs.
