Yes. When severe Melbourne storms cause immediate damage to your tiled roof, our priority is securing the property. We provide professional make-safe services, including immediate tarping, and detailed condition reports for your insurer.

Storm damage often means shattered tiles, torn valley irons, or displaced ridge caps. Once the immediate threat is handled and the insurance is approved, we perform a permanent [4-stage tiled restoration](/services/tiled-restoration) focusing on flexi-pointing and targeted structural repairs, ensuring your home is watertight for the next storm. Learn more about our [Insurance Roof Repairs & Make-Safe services](/services/insurance-roof-repairs).
