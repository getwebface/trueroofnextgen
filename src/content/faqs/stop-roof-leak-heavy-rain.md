To temporarily stop a tiled roof leak during heavy rain, place a bucket under the drip and use a broom handle to poke a small 5mm drainage hole in the ceiling plaster. This releases pooled water, preventing a 100kg ceiling collapse before emergency roofers arrive.

Never climb on a wet, tiled roof during an active storm. Attempting a DIY tarp job is extremely dangerous. Instead, manage the water flow internally to protect your property and contents. Once the storm passes or conditions are safe, our [Melbourne](/locations/melbourne) emergency team can provide professional make-safe tarping and a comprehensive condition report.

If your roof has sustained significant storm damage, our [Insurance Roof Repairs](/services/insurance-roof-repairs) service will secure the property before we proceed with a permanent [tiled roof restoration](/services/tiled-restoration).
