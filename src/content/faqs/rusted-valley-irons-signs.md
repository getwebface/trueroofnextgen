Look for visible orange rust lines along the roof valleys, flaking metal, or water pooling after heavy rain. Inside, check for brown water stains on your ceiling directly below a roof intersection.

When valley irons deteriorate, they often cause slow, hidden leaks that can damage timber framing and plasterboard.
