A professional tiled roof restoration in Melbourne will last between 15 to 20 years. This lifespan depends on the quality of materials used, proper high-pressure cleaning, flexible pointing application, and the impact of Melbourne's harsh UV and storm weather conditions.

To ensure maximum longevity, True Roof performs a comprehensive [4-stage tiled restoration process](/services/tiled-restoration). We start with thorough [high-pressure cleaning](/services/cleaning) to remove abrasive moss and lichen. Crucially, we secure your roof using advanced [ridge cap re-bedding and pointing](/services/ridge-cap-rebedding-pointing) that flexes with temperature changes, rather than cracking like traditional mortar.

Whether your home faces strong winds in [Craigieburn](/locations/craigieburn) or unpredictable weather across [Melbourne](/locations/melbourne), investing in a high-quality restoration adds decades of life to your roof without the extreme cost of a full replacement.
