A professionally applied, multi-coat protective membrane system on concrete tiles typically lasts between 10 to 15 years in Melbourne's harsh climate. Proper preparation, including high-pressure cleaning and deep-penetrating primers, ensures the coating bonds permanently, protecting your roof from water ingress and UV degradation.

Unlike standard exterior paint, which quickly flakes and peels under the Australian sun, a specialized membrane system is designed specifically for porous concrete tiles. This is Stage 4 of our [tiled restoration process](/services/tiled-restoration). By filling micro-cracks and providing a flexible, watertight shield, [concrete tile sealing](/services/concrete-tile-sealing) effectively resets the lifespan of your roof. Regular inspections and cleaning will maximize the longevity of this protective coating, saving you from expensive structural water damage in the long run.
