Sarking is a protective reflective foil membrane installed directly beneath your roof tiles. It serves two critical functions:

1.  **Weather Protection:** It acts as a secondary barrier, stopping wind-driven rain, fine dust, and condensation from entering your ceiling cavity if water manages to get past the tiles.
2.  **Thermal Insulation:** The reflective surface significantly improves your home’s energy efficiency by reflecting radiant heat away in the summer and trapping warmth in the winter.

Over several decades, standard sarking can degrade—becoming brittle, tearing, or sagging. When this happens, even a perfectly tiled roof can experience hidden leaks during severe Melbourne squalls.

Rather than ignoring failing sarking, our [targeted sarking repair and replacement](/services/sarking-repairs) seamlessly integrates with our 4-Stage Restoration Process. If we identify damaged sarking during your restoration audit, our experienced technicians will safely remove the affected tiles, replace the compromised foil with modern, high-grade heavy-duty sarking, and meticulously relay the tiles. This ensures your home is completely protected from the inside out, without the need for extreme structural overhauls.
