## 2024-05-15 - Northern Growth Corridor Housing Trend
**Learning:** Learned that properties in the Northern growth corridor (e.g., Craigieburn, Mickleham) built roughly 20-25 years ago primarily feature concrete tiles. These open plain areas are heavily exposed to high winds, causing faster degradation of mortar compared to sheltered suburbs.
**Action:** When targeting Northern corridor suburbs on the open plains, tailor the `weatherOverrides` and body copy to emphasize wind damage and the necessity of advanced flexi-pointing to lock ridge caps tight.

## 2026-03-05 - Eastern Suburbs Tree Canopy Impact
