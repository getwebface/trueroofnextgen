## 2025-02-28 - Testimonial Next to Lead Form
**Learning:** Placing a verified, highly localized 5-star testimonial immediately adjacent to the lead capture form serves as powerful third-party validation exactly at the highest point of friction, reducing user anxiety and increasing form completion rates. The existing `#quote` section on `index.astro` is split into two columns (text + PromoCard on the left, Form on the right on desktop), making the left column the perfect location for this social proof without disrupting the form layout itself. Using the standard codebase `glass-card` combined with brand orange accents (`--color-brand-orange`) integrates it seamlessly into the existing design system.
**Action:** When adding conversion elements like testimonials, always place them as close to the call-to-action or form as possible, utilizing existing UI patterns (`glass-card`, `badge`) and brand colors to maintain visual consistency while maximizing psychological impact.
