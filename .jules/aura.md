## 2024-10-25 - Contextual Trust Signals Build Reassurance **Learning:** Dynamic adjustments to static credibility indicators (like the TrustBar) based on high `urgency` effectively shift user perception from general reputation to immediate safety. Emphasizing "Fully Insured for Storm Damage" during a storm provides psychological comfort when anxiety is highest. **Action:** Apply this pattern to other trust elements: tie badges or guarantees directly to the current atmospheric threat rather than leaving them generic.
## 2024-10-25 - Contextual UI Destructuring **Learning:** When injecting `WeatherContext` properties like `urgency` into `.astro` components, it's critical to ensure the property is destructured safely (e.g., `const urgency = weatherContext?.urgency || 1;`) rather than referencing it implicitly, preventing `ReferenceError`s during SSR. **Action:** Always verify variable declarations when passing context data to UI elements.
