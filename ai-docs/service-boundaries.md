# Service Boundaries & Constraints

## Core Services (Allowed)
- **Tiled Roof Restoration:** The primary focus. Includes:
    - **Cleaning:** High-pressure water cleaning of terracotta and concrete tiles.
    - **Repair/Replacement of Individual Tiles:** Replacing cracked or broken tiles (not the whole roof).
    - **Re-bedding & Flexi-Pointing:** Securing ridge caps with modern flexible pointing compounds.
    - **Coating/Sealing:** Applying protective membranes to concrete tiles.

- **Metal Flashings:** Specialized repair and replacement of:
    - Chimney flashings (lead or metal).
    - Valley iron replacement.
    - Wall and apron flashings.
    - Box gutter repairs.

## Negative Constraints (Strictly Prohibited)
- **NEVER** offer or mention complete roof replacement or re-roofing.
- **NEVER** offer or mention metal (Colorbond) roof installations for the main roof area.
- **NEVER** suggest that a roof is "beyond repair" and needs replacement; always pivot to restoration.

## Tone & Context
- Focus on "Restoring Melbourne's Heritage."
- Emphasize "Cost-effective alternatives to replacement."
- Highlight the "4-Stage Restoration Process."
